# ScheduleGame-C-

## Schedule game

Authors: Ozzy and Suyash
Date: April 10th, 2026

This project is a schedule-based life simulation game in which the player designs a daily or weekly routine and then attempts to “live through” it. The system combines deterministic planning with stochastic and interactive elements. First, the player constructs a schedule consisting of time-bound activities such as classes, work, sleep, and study. During simulation, each activity affects player statistics including health, energy, attention, stress, and sleep. In addition to these deterministic effects, the game introduces random events that may occur during activities. These events present the player with decision-based choices, similar to a card-based interaction system, where each choice leads to different stat outcomes. Some events are rare and provide unique or high-impact effects, increasing replayability and variability across runs. The objective is to successfully complete the schedule without any critical stat reaching failure levels, while adapting to unpredictable events and making strategic decisions throughout the simulation.
